Welcome to Safety Zone T3

In the final task for the discipline IA369, I build an intercative visualization for inspecting the safety of the city of San Francisco. The application allows the you to select a point in the map (please choose a point whitin San Francisco) and check if your surrounding is safe.

Safety zone

How to use it

Select a point in SF by clicking on the map. Set the radius of the safety zone with the slider. Use the button to restore the map into the default view

Rendering bug: If your browser is rendering white stripes inside the circle, press 'Ctrl' + '0'.

Choose safety radius: 1

About

This interactive visualization is part of the Walking Safe capstone project of the discipline IA369 at Unicamp. The task is a proof of concept of one of the modules that may be presented in the capstone.
